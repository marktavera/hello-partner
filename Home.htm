Who is Gian Estera?

Gian Baldoza Estera or Gian for short is a student of De La Salle College of Saint Benilde taking up Bachelor of Science Major in Information Systems. She is still 19 years old young and she was born on November 25, 1997. She grew up in the middle east with her family. Her father's name is Manuel Estera who grew up in Baguio who is an Overseas Worker in Muscat, Oman and her mother's name is Ma. Gina Estera who is also an Overseas Worker in Muscat, Oman. She only has one sister named Mikaela Bianca Estera who is just graduated last 2015 with a degree in Communication Arts. She used to work at Kidzania, BGC as a supervisor and is now working as a journalist.

She finished her primary education in 3 schools namely St. Louise de Marillac in Albay, St. Louise University in Baguio, and Philippine School Sultanate of Oman. Likewise, she finished her Secondary education in 3 schools namely Azzan Bin Qais International School, American British Academy, Philippine School Sultanate of Oman. She is very diligent when it comes to her academics. She studies hard in order for her to have high grades and achieve her dreams.

She is very active in terms of her Co-curricular activities. She is a part of the PS Choral so don't be surprised if she has an amazing singing voice. She was also a part of the Student Council in her school she held the position of Class Representative and Treasurer so you can trust her in keeping your money safe and also display good leadership skills. Furthermore she is very artistic and becoming a Pacesetter and their yearbook content and photographer. Lastly she is very althletic that's why she is a part of their school's Volleyball Varsity Team for 3 years.

Some of her hobbies and interest are listening to music, reading different kinds of books. She is also fond of baking foods and desserts, and lastly she is very adventurous that she likes having outdoor activities and that may be the root of her interest in photography.
